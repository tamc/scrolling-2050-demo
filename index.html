The UK 2050 Calculator

The UK's parliament requires the UK government to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 80%, 1990—2050. There are many ways that this could be done, but no approach is universally popular.

This page presents 42 sets of choices about what could be physically changed about the UK, allowing you to see what might need to be done to reduce emissions, what makes a big difference and what doesn't, and where the trade-offs lie. If you want to dive further into the detail, all the underlying calculations are available in an open souce Excel spreadsheet.
